Katherine Schlief (née Hoffman) 1833 - 1916

The daughter of Theobald (Tentative) Hoffman and Elisabetha Philippina (Tentative)Theobald, Katherine was the 3 times great-grandmother of Jay Hannah, was born in Germany (Bavaria) on May 9, 1833 and married Samuel Schlief (with whom she had 10 children: Kathryn Amelia, Katherine, Louis, Mary, Samuel , JR, Christena, Elizabeth, George, William and John) in New York around 1852.

She died on Mar 23, 1916 in Fairfield, Iowa1.
