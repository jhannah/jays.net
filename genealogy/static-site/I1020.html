Nellie Elsie Harshbarger (née Bunker) 1891 - 1981

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The youngest of 7 children of William Bunker and Rosella Virginia 'Rosa' Canady, Nellie was the first cousin three-times-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in Jackson Twp., Henry Co., IA on Feb 3, 1891 and married Albert Harshbarger (with whom she had 2 children: Harold Keith and Wayne) in Salem, Henry Co., Iowa on Feb 5, 1913.

She died on Apr 29, 1981 and was buried in Salem South Cemetery after Apr 29, 1981.
