George William Bunker 1869 - 1962

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The 2nd of 5 children of Jonathan Bunker and Julia Collins, George was the first cousin three-times-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in Jackson Twp., Henry Co., IA on Mar 25, 1869 and married Finetta Byers (with whom he had 2 children: Ralph Waldo and Merle Roscoe) in Salem, IA on Oct 1, 1893.

He died on Feb 12, 1962 in Mt. Pleasant, Iowa, USA.
