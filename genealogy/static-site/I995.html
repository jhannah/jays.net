Leo Robert Hannah 1898 - 1984

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The older of 2 children of Robert Hannah and Edith Tempel, Leo was the first cousin three-times-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in Jackson Twp. Henry Co., IA on Aug 12, 1898 and married Gertrude Mattern (with whom he had 1 child, Maurice Marlo) in Donnellson, IA on Dec 19, 1917.

He died on Apr 11, 1984 in Klein Hospital, Burlington, IA and was buried in Forest Home Cem., Mt. Pleasant, IA after Apr 11, 1984.

Mt. Pleasant News, April 12, 1984

Leo Hannah

Leo Hannah, 85, Burlington, died at Klein Hospital, Burlington,Wednesday, April 11, 1984.

He was born Aug. 12, 1898, the son of Robert and Edith Temple Hannah.He married Gertrude Mattern on Dec. 19,1917, in Donnellson.

Mr. Hannah was a farmer and worked at the I.A.A.P. he was a 50-yearMason Lodge No. 17 of Salem and a member of Fraternal Order of Eagles No.150.

Surviving are his wife; one son, Maurice, San Bernadino, Calif. onesister, Mrs. Frances Froid, St. Petersburg, Fla. two grandchildren;andsix great-grandchildren.

Services will be held at Lunning Chapel, Burlington, at 10 a.m.Friday. The Rev. Richard Gilbert will officiate with interment at ForestHome Cemetery. Masonic services will be held at 7 p.m. Thursday at thechapel with visitation following.

A memorial has been established for the Klein Hospital recreationaldepartment.
