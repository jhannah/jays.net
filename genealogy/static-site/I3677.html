Salem News, Nov. 22, 1951

THOMAS EMBREE GIBSON

Thomas Embree Gibson was born northwest of Salem on June 24, 1896,theson of Isaac and Anna Gibson. He went with his parents and family toIndian territory now Oklahoma by covered wagon. At this time he wasonly3 years old.

He returned to Iowa when a young man and went to Wesleyan college inMt. Pleasant. After completing college, he returned to Oklahoma with hisbrother, Allen, where he was married to Louisa Gale. To this union adaughter, now Margaret Gibson Liddell was born.

The remainder of his life was spent in Oklahoma, except for a few lateryears spent on his old homesite near Salem. He is survived by hisdaughter and a bother Allen.

Funeral services were held in the Friend's church, the pastor Rev.Arthur Moon officiating. Music was by Mrs. Ethel Garretson and burialwas in the Friends cemetery. Pall bearers were Allie Hodson, RayDavidson, John McMasters, A. J. Lee, Jess Friend and J. W. Moxley.
