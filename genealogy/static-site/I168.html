Christina Sappenfield (née Grimes) 1792 - 1858

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The child of (John) Charles Grimes and Barbara Frank, Christina was the 5 times great-grandmother of Jay Hannah, was born in Rowan Co., North Carolina on Nov 26, 1792 and married (Teis) Matthias Sappenfield (with whom she had 15 children: Jacob, Absolom, Huber, Andrew, Mathias, Mariah B, Sarah, Eliza E, Christina, Felix, John M, Mathias, Charles W, Eusebuis Marion and Aurelia) in Rowan Co. on Aug 25, 1811.

She died on Jul 31, 1858 in Parke Co., Indiana1.
