Matilda Long (née Macy) 1832 - 1915

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The child of Seth Macy and Rebecca Simmons, , Matilda was the 3 times great-grandmother of Jay Hannah, was born in Randolph Co., IN on Mar 4, 1832 and married Benjamin Long (with whom she had 4 children: Etta Jane, Rebecca Macy, John Franklin and Lucinda Ann "Anna") in Jackson Twp., Henry Co., IA on Aug 7, 1856.

She died on Apr 25, 1915 in Salem, IA.
