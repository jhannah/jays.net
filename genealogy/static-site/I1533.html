Ellen May Long (née Armer) 1860 - 1942

The 2nd of 9 children of James Armer and Maria GambleEllen was the great-great-grandmother of Jay Hannah, was born in Indiana Co., PA on Aug 1, 1860 and married John Long (with whom she had 8 children: Arthur Macy, Eva Matilda, Perry, Ida Maria, Grover Jay, Francis May, Anna and John Byron) in near Salem, Henry Co., IA on Oct 30, 1878.
