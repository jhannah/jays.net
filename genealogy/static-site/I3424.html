James Lindley Joy 1895 - 1968

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The eldest of 12 children of Reuben Joy and Elizabeth 'Lizzie' McIntosh, James was the second cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in Hays, KS on Dec 6, 1895 and married Anna Angelina 'Lina' Joy (with whom he had 5 children: Thelma Elizabeth, Cecil Lindley, Helen, Frances Elaine and Norma Irene) in Hays on Oct 12, 1921.

He died on May 27, 1968 in Ellis Co., KS and was buried in Mount Allen Cemetery, Hays, KS after May 27, 1968.
