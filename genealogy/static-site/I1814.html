Forrest Franklin Donaldson 1892 - 1976

The eldest of 6 children of Samuel Donaldson and Katie BunkerForrest was the second cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in Salem, Henry Co., Iowa on Apr 1, 1892. He was married twice - to Ruth Spray (on Jul 19, 1921 in Burlington, Des Moines Co., IA) Nellie Fuller (on Jun 15, 1947 in Mt. pleasant, Henry Co., Iowa, following the death of Ruth on Nov 30, 1946).

He died on Mar 28, 1976 in Mt. Pleasant, Henry Co. and was buried in Salem South Cemetery after Mar 28, 1976.

Salem News 7-20-1922

Spray- Donaldson

The marriage of Miss Ruth Spray and Mr. Forrest Donaldson occurredWednesday, July 19 at the home of Rev. J.W .Stine, Burlington. Followingthe ceremony a wedding supper was served at the home of the groomssister in Burlington, Mrs. Jess Kerr. The bride is the daughter of Mrs.Margaret Spray, the groom being the son of Mr. and Mrs. S.F. Donaldson.Both are most excellent young people and have hosts of friends. Theywill reside on their farm near Hillsboro. The news joins their friendsin extending congratulations.
