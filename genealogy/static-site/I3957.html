Millicent Bogue (née Hiatt) 1809 - 1896

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The child of Silas Hiatt and Anna Clary, Millicent was the first cousin five-times-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in Guilford Co., NC on Aug 30, 1809 and married Benjamin Bogue (with whom she had 15 children: Malinda Hiatt, Anna Hiatt, Silas, Huldah, Samuel, Henry, Joshua, Allen, Aseneth Hiatt, Newby, Mary Ellen, Joseph Dickinson, Benjamin F. "Frank", Emily and Elvirene) in Wayne Co., IN on May 29, 1828.

She died on Mar 22, 1896 in Emporia, Lynn Co., KS.
