Mira Bunker (née Dillingham) 1809 - 1859

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The child of John Dillingham and Mary Covill, Mira was the 3 times great-grandmother of Jay Hannah, was born in Mt. Vernon, Knox Co., Ohio or Coeymans, Albany Co., NY on Jun 30, 1809 and married Robert Bunker (with whom she had 9 children: Mary Dillingham, Hannah Rosetta, Ann Sample, Jonathan Smith, Elizabeth Hutchinson, Rachel Joy, Gulielma Myra "Elma", William Kerr and Sarah Angeline) in Mt. Vernon, Knox Co., Ohio in 1829.

She died on Dec 26, 1859 in Ackworth, Warren Co., IA.
