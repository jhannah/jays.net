Mt. Pleasant News Sept. 9, 1959

Mrs. L. A. Bunker

Taken By Death

Mrs. Margaret Bunker, age 83, died Tuesday afternoon in the Henrycounty Memorial hospital after illness for the past sever years. She wasthe daughter of Samuel and Elizabeth Williams Milligan.

On November 30, 1875, she was born in Henry county, Iowa, near Salem.She was united in marriage with L. A. (Bert) Bunker on February 25, 1903,who preceded her in death on March 4, 1957. One child died in infancy.

She was a member of the Congregational Church in Salem and has been aresident in Mt. Pleasant for the past three years, moving form Salem.Mrs. Bunker was the last of her family. Surviving are several nieces,nephews and great nieces and nephews.

Funeral services will be at 3 p.m. Saturday, at the Crane Funeral Home.The Rev. Robert Penrod, pastor of the Salem Congregational Church willofficiate. Interment will be in the Salem Cemetery. Friends may call atthe funeral home as they wish.
