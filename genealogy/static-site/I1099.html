Celesta Knight (née Byers) 1876 - 1937

The 4th of 12 children of Perry Byers and Ruth Scarborough, Celesta was the great-great-aunt of Jay Hannah, was born in Henry Co., IA on May 22, 1876 and was married twice - to Charles Annear Milton Knight (on Mar 10, 1896). She had 5 children: Verlin Miles with Milton Joseph; and Wilbur, Hollis, Eldon and Loren Ilif with Charles Arthur.

She died on Jan 17, 1937 in Dawson, IA.
