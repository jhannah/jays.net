Waunita Cox 1920 - 1920

The 2nd of 4 children of Walter Cox and Ada Donaldson, Waunita was the third cousin once-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in Salem, Henry Co., Iowa on Jan 27, 1920.

She died on Jan 30, 1920 in Salem and was buried in Salem South Cemetery after Jan 30, 1920.

Notes

Salem News February 5,1920

DEATHS OF THE WEEK

COX

Waunita, infant daughter of Walter and Ada Cox, born January 27, 1920,died January 30, 1920.

A brief sojourn, but whose mission unsullied by earthly contact, whocan measure?

The little flower of Paradise slipped away unaccountably, withoutwarning, it passed to immortal arms above, without brushing the skirts ofwoe.

Many sympathizing friends attended the funeral at the home Sunday at1:30. All was in keeping with the occasion. Scripture by Miss Baker,Duets by Mary Smith and Helen McMaster, and remarks by T. R. Gray.

Burial in Salem Friends cemetery.
