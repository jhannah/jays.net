Joel Hiatt 1770 - 1857

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The eldest of 11 children of William Hiatt and Charity Williams, Joel was the fifth times great-uncle of Jay Hannah, was born in Guilford Co. , NC on Feb 15, 1770 and was married twice - to Mary Uthank (on Jun 18, 1794 in New Garden MM, Guilford Co., NC) Rhoda (Davis) Mace (on Nov 21, 1832 in New Garden MM, Wayne Co., IN). He had 1 child with Mary Uthank, Allen.

He died on May 3, 1857 in Henry Co., IA and was buried in Salem South Cemetery after May 3, 1857.
