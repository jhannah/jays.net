Emma Mae Lund (née Collins) 1885 - 1976

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The 6th of 7 children of William Collins and Rachel Bunker, Emma was the first cousin three-times-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in Henry Co., IA on May 30, 1885 and had 2 children with Arthur Lund: Lyle William and Dorothy May.

She died on Jan 26, 1976 in Seattle, King Co., WA.
