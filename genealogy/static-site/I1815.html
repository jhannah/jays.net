Hazel Blanche Stone (née Donaldson) 1894 - 1990

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The 2nd of 6 children of Samuel Donaldson and Katie Bunker, Hazel was the second cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in Salem, Henry Co., Iowa on Aug 2, 1894 and married John Stone (with whom she had 3 children: Katyryn, Lucille Mae and Helen Rebecca) in Mt. Pleasant, Henry Co., Iowa on Jun 22, 1927.

She died on May 15, 1990 in Albuquerque, Bernalillo Co. New Mexico.
