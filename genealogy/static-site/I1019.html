Clyde Franklin Bunker 1886 - 1969

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The 6th of 7 children of William Bunker and Rosella Virginia 'Rosa' Canady, Clyde was the first cousin three-times-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in Jackson Twp., Henry Co., IA on Feb 18, 1886 and married Lola Parkins (with whom he had 1 child, Eva Marie) in Salem, Henry Co., Iowa on May 19, 1909.

He died on Jul 25, 1969 in Henry Co., IA and was buried in Salem South Cemetery after Jul 25, 1969.

Salem News February 10, 1916

Clyde Bunker and wife have moved onto the A.C. Wolf place west oftown. Edgar Long of near Augusta will move onto the Long farm vacated byMr. Bunker....

Salem News Sept. 22, 1921

"BARN BURNED

The large new barn on the Clyde Bunker farm was struck by lightningduring the storm Tuesday night and completely destroyed by fire. All thehorses were saved but one dog ran back into the flames and was lost.Thiswas a new barn, justbuilt. It is a wonder we don't have more fires,with almost daily thunder storms we are having lately."

"Mr. Bunker informs us the loss on his barn was nearly covered byinsurance.".
