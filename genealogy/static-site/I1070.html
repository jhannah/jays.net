Mt. Pleasant News May ,1929

MISS FLORENCE HANNAH

BECOMES BRIDE OF

HAMILTON D. WILLIAMS

At nine o'clock, Wednesday morning, May first, 1929, Miss FlorenceJ.Hannah, daughter of Mrs. John R. Hannah, and Mr. Hamilton D. Williams,son of Mr. and Mrs. Don E. Williams, were joined in wedlock at the homeof Frank E. Weston, pastor of the First Baptist church. They wereaccompanied by Miss Mildred Williams, sister of the bridegroom, and Mr.Wayne Hannah, brother of the bride, who were the ascribing witnesses. Thebeautiful single ring service was used. After the vows were spoken andthe marriage returns filled in, the happy couple left for a trip toKansas City and other points west.

Both young folks are well and favorably known here. Both are nativesof Henry county and are members of two of its best families. The bridewas graduated form Mt. Pleasant high school in the class of '27. Thebridegroom is a salesman for the Kansas City Star, and is successfulinhis work. Both have the best wishes of their many friends for a happywedded life.
