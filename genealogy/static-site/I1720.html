Notes

The Salem News, April 13, 1972

Mrs. Lola Bunker

Mrs. Lola Bunker, 83, died about 8:30 a.m. Wednesday, April 12,1972, inthe Henry County Memorial Hospital.

She was born April 28, 1888, at Salem the daughter of Levi and HannaTribby Parkins.

She was married to Clyde Bunker at Salem who preceded her in deathin1968.

Mrs. Bunker was a member of the Salem Congregational Church. She andher late husband operated a hardware store in Salem for many years.

Surviving are one daughter, Mrs. La Verne (Eva) Judd, Mt. Pleasant, sixgrandchildren, five great grandchildren, five great grandchildren andmany nieces and nephews.

Funeral services will be held Friday, April 14 at 2 p.m. form eh SalemCongregational Church. The Rev. Edmund Neubauer, pastor of the church,and the Rev. Waylen Brown will officiate.

Interment will be in the Salem South Cemetery.

Friends may call as they desire after 7 p.m. Wednesday atBeatty-Jennings Funeral Chapel in Mt. Pleasant. The body will be takento the church at 10 a.m. Friday to lie in state.
