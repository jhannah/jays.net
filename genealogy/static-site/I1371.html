Rudolph Byers 1848 - 1910

The child of John Byers and Mary Daily, Rudolph was the three times great-uncle of Jay Hannah, was born in Huntington, IN on Apr 24, 1848 and married Ida Edwards (with whom he had 4 children: Edward, James, Joseph and Rolly (Hugh)) in Mt. Pleasant, IA on Oct 4, 1871.

He died on Feb 4, 1910 in Imboden, Ark.

Mt. Pleasant Weekly News March 30, 1910
Word has been received of the death of Mr. Rudolph Byers at Emboden,Ark. He formerly lived here and is a brother of Mrs. Margaret ShortandPerry Byers, of this vicinity, and an uncle of Mr. John Short of thiscity. He leaves otherrelatives in and near Salem. He was about 70years of age and had been a great sufferer for years with paralysis. Heleaves a wife and several children.
