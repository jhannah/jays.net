Barbara Seaton (née Bushman) 1795 - 1874

The daughter of Henry Bushman and Libby HousemanBarbara was the 4 times great-grandmother of Jay Hannah, was born in Martinsburg, Berkeley Co., WV on Sep 22, 1795 and married Ebenezer Seaton (with whom she had 13 children: Martha, John, David, Elizabeth, Mary, Rebecca, Allen, William Henry, Benjamin F, Sarah, Catherine J, Sibby Ann Sophia and Ebenezer P) in Tate Twp, Clermont, OH on Mar 13, 1817, which is also where she died on May 24, 1874.
