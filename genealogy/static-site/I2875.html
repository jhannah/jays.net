Ida May Wirtz (née McIntosh) 1872 - 1918

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The 3rd of 6 children of James McIntosh and Cordelia Byers, Ida was the first cousin three-times-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in IA on Aug 18, 1872 and married Herman Wirtz (with whom she had 8 children: Herman William, John Henry, Mary Ann, Cordelia Elizabeth, Clarence Oliver, Samuel Guy, Gaylord Otto and George Elic) on Nov 10, 1895.

She died on Dec 25, 1918 in Wakita, Grant Co., OK.
