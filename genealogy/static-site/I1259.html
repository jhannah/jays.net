Mt. Pleasant News April 1977

Mrs. Jay Long

Mrs. Jay Long, 85, of Salem, died Wednesday, April 20, 1977 at WeiserCommunity Hospital at Weiser, Idaho, following a short illness.

Luella long, daughter of Marion and Sophia Schisler Miller, was bornDec. 1,1891, at Vermont, Ill.

Mrs. Long moved to Salem, Iowa when a small child, attended schoolthere, and spent most of her life in Salem. She was a member of theSalem Methodist Church. On July 19, 1910, she was married to Jay Long atMt. Pleasant. He died on May 16, 1963.

Mrs. Long is survived by four daughters: Mrs. Erma Serles, Hinsdale,Ill., Mrs. Don (Dorothy) Watson, Cambridge, Idaho; Mrs. Hjalmar (Jean)Monson, Mt. Pleasant, Mrs. Conrad (Liberta) Taylor, Roseville, Ill.;twosons; Harold Edwin Long, New Orleans, La., and Robert Jay Long, LakeMills; eight grandsons; one granddaughter; three great-grandchildren;several nieces and nephews.

She was preceded in death by her parents, her husband, two daughters,Vivian McDowell and Doris Ellen, who died in infancy, one half brotherRalph Parr, one half sister, Amanda Parr, one granddaughter, Verna JeanSerles, and one great-granddaughter, Jennifer Hannah.

Funeral services will be held Sunday, April 24, 2:30 p.m., at theBeatty-Jennings Funeral Home in Salem. The Rev. Lewis Savage willofficiate with interment in Salem East Cemetery.

Family will receive friends from 7 to 8 p.m. Saturday, April 23 atthefuneral home.
