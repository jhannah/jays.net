Johannes Andersson 1791 - 1869

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The son of Anders Larson and Maria Hansdotter, Johannes was the 3 times great-grandfather of Jay Hannah, was born in Timmele Parish Sweden on Nov 27, 1791 and was married twice - to Anna Nyberg (on Dec 2, 1821 in Hossna) Lovisa Lonnerberg (on Jul 5, 1834 in Karthemmet, Hossna, Alvsberg, Sweden, following the death of Anna Lisa Gudmundsdotter on Feb 20, 1832). He had 11 children: Carl August, Johan Adolph, Maria Charlotta(Elisabeth), Emma Christina, Svante, Emma Christina and Salomon with Lovisa; and Josef, Anders Gustaf, Fredrika and Johan Adolph with Anna Lisa Gudmundsdotter. In 1868, he lived in Hossna household record Page 1871. Moved in 1821. (Moved).

He died on Oct 5, 1869 in Karthemmet farm, Hossna, Elfsberg, Vastergotland, Sweden1.
