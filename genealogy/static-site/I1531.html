Benjamin Thomas Long 1834 - 1915

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The son of Thomas Long and Perlina Long, Benjamin was the 3 times great-grandfather of Jay Hannah, was born in Indiana on Nov 9, 1834 and married Matilda Macy (with whom he had 4 children: Etta Jane, Rebecca Macy, John Franklin and Lucinda Ann "Anna") in Jackson Twp., Henry Co., IA on Aug 7, 1856.

He died on Feb 3, 1915 in Salem, IA.
