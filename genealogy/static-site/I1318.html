Esther Ruth Richardson (née Hannah) 1903 - 1994

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The youngest of 6 children of John Hannah and Viola Courter, Esther was the first cousin three-times-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in Henry Co., Jackson Twp., in a log cabin on Feb 11, 1903 and married Robert Richardson (with whom she had 3 children: Dorothy Ruth, Helen Verlee and Robert Floyd) in Harlan Courter home in Winfield, IA on Sep 30, 1923.

She died in 1994.
