Mary Dillingham Shannon (née Bunker) 1831 - 1869

The eldest of 9 children of Robert Bunker and Mira Dillingham, Mary was the three times great-aunt of Jay Hannah, was born in Hudson, Columbia Co., New York on Jan 8, 1831 and married William Monroe 'Billie' Shannon (with whom she had 8 children: Frances Josephine, James Edwin, Almira Catherine "Katy", Margaret Ann, Clara Elizabeth, Minerva Eldora "Dora", Martha Jane "Mattie" and Mary) in Warren Co., IA on Apr 10, 1853.

She died on May 18, 1869 in Henry Co., Iowa, USA.
