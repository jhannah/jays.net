John Alexander McIntosh 1875 - 1948

The 4th of 6 children of James McIntosh and Cordelia Byers, John was the first cousin three-times-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in Green Co., IA on Feb 24, 1875 and married Rose Helena 'Lena' Haddock (with whom he had 10 children: Mrs. John, James W, , Fred, Mrs. O. L, Lawrence, Frank, Mrs. A. F, Mrs. Tom and Mrs. Wayne) on Jan 26, 1898.

He died in 1948.

Hays Daily News Dec. 28, 1948
John McIntosh, 73
Dies At Home Here
John Alexander McIntosh, 73, died late yesterday afternoon at his homeon East 17 th street after an illness of four months.
Mr. McIntosh was born February 24, 1875 in Green County, Ia., and camewith his parents, Cordelia and James McIntosh to Ellis county two yearslater. On January 26, 1898 he was married to Rose Helena Haddock andthey had ten children,one of whom died as an infant.
They are surviving, the widow an these children; Mrs. John Simonson ofOlympia, Wash., James W. McIntosh, Oregon City Ore. Fred McIntosh,Oregon City, Ore. Mrs. O. L. Hunley, San Diego, Calif. LawrenceMcIntosh, Collyer; FrankMcIntosh; Mrs. A. F. Debes; Mrs. Tom Payne andMrs. Wayne Cook of Hays. There is one brother, George of Hays and asister Mrs. Clara Balls of Hays. There are 83 grandchildren and threegreat grandchildren.
Funeral services will be at 10:30 o'clock Thursday morning at theMorford Mortuary.
