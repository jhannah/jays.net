Notes

Mt. Pleasant Journal March 9, 1882

DIED

In Jackson township, Feb. 12, 1882, of paralysis of the brain, Mary,daughter of John and Katie Hannah.

Mary was a young lady of quiet deportment and studious habits and byher genial disposition had gained the esteem of all who knew her andshewill be sadly missed in the home and the social circle of thiscommunity. The funeral discourse delivered by Rev. J. S. Ferguson, atDonaldson chapel was attended by a large concourse of friends. Thuspassed away in the morning of life one who gave promise of muchusefulness. The grief stricken parents have the sympathy of the entirecommunity in this hour of trial.
