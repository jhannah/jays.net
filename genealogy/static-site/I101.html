Fredricka Charlotta Johansson (née Anderson) 1846 - 1927

Fredricka was the great-great-grandmother of Jay Hannah, was born in Hallstad, Elfsborgs, Vastergotland, Sweden on Oct 21, 1846 and married Svante Johansson (with whom she had 9 children: Carl Adolph, John Albert, Charley Robert, Hendrick Edvard, Maria Zeraphia, Wilhelm Solomen, Anna Lovina, Otto Picard and Edith (Idet) Tekla) in Rock Island, Illinois on Aug 29, 1868.

She died on Dec 31, 1927 in Des Moines, Polk Co., IA1.
