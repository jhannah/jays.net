Unknown Newspaper Nov 1960

Mrs. Maude Byers

Taken By Death

Mrs. Winona Maude Byers, 76, died Tuesday at the Millspaugh NursingHome where she had been for the past week.

She was born February 1, 1884 in Jackson Township, the daughter ofNathan P. Nicholson and Florabelle (Prottsman) Nicholson.

She was married to Alfred Byers on June 23, 1909, was a resident ofSalem all of her life, and a member of the Congregational church.

Mrs. Byers was predeceased by her husband on October 11, 1941 and oneson Verle Byers on May 5, 1951, and also by her parents and one brother.

She is survived by one brother, Russell Nicholson of Hillsboro.

Funeral services will be Friday at 2:00 p.m. at the Waterman FuneralHome in Salem. The Rev. Robert Penrod will officiate.

Burial will be in the Salem South cemetery.
