Cora Belle Oakley (née Hannah) 1900 - 1974

The 5th of 6 children of John Hannah and Viola Courter, Cora was the first cousin three-times-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in Henry Co., Jackson Twp. in a log cabin on Nov 23, 1900. She was married twice - to Paul Walker (on May 8, 1923) George Oakley (on Jul 2, 1940 in Morning Sun, IA).

She died on Jul 12, 1974 in Morning Sun, Bur. Elmwood Cmty., IA.
