The Hannah ancestors were of Irish origin, and the Fultons were probably of Scottish descent.

James was by trade a shoemaker, but after his marriage engaged solely in agriculture, removing at a very early date to Brown County, Ohio, where he entered lands, built a house, and had a family of eight children before he removed to Clermont County in the same state.
