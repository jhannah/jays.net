Sarah Pinkham Mosher (née Bunker) 1805 - 1887

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The 6th of 7 children of Jonathan Bunker and Comfort Smith, Sarah was the four times great-aunt of Jay Hannah, was born in Hudson, Columbia Co., New York on Jul 29, 1805 and married Asa Mosher (with whom she had 9 children: Stephen, Jonathan B, Hannah, Thomas T, Susannah B, Elijah P, Sara Angeline, Mary Ann and Obadiah A) in Deleware Co., OH on Jul 6, 1825.

She died on Dec 13, 1887 in Liberty Center, Warren Co., IA.
