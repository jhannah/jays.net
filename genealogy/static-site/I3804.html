Allen Hiatt 1795 - 1885

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The child of Joel Hiatt and Mary Uthank, Allen was the first cousin five-times-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in Guilford, NC on Jun 6, 1795 and married Rhoda Hunt (with whom he had 6 children: Susanna, Gulielma, John Milton, Addison, Anna Mary and Minerva E) in Rowan, NC on Dec 19, 1818.

He died on Nov 2, 1885 in Salem, Henry Co., IA and was buried in Salem South Cemetery after Nov 2, 1885.
