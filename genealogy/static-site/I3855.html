Celestia May Frary (née Hockett) 1871 - 1953

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The eldest of 4 children of Nathan Hockett and Avaline CrosbieCelestia was the first cousin three-times-removed on the mother's side of Jay Hannah, was born in Salem, Henry Co., IA on Mar 12, 1871 and married Jesse Frary (with whom she had 2 children: Oliver Max and Jerusha Maine) in Henry Co., IA on May 1, 1889.

She died in Feb 1953 in Greeley, Weld Co., CO.
