Lovisa Andersson (née Lonnerberg) 1803 - 1867

The child of Anders Lonnerberg and Maria Olofsdotter, Lovisa was the 3 times great-grandmother of Jay Hannah, was born in Ulricehamn, Sweden on Nov 15, 1803 and married Johannes Andersson (with whom she had 7 children: Carl August, Johan Adolph, Maria Charlotta(Elisabeth), Emma Christina, Svante, Emma Christina and Salomon) in Karthemmet, Hossna, Alvsberg, Sweden on Jul 5, 1834.

She died on Feb 11, 1867 in Karthemmet farm, Hossna, Elfsberg, Vastergotland, Sweden1.
