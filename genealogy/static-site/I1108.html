Charity McVey (née Scarborough) 1859 - 1947

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The child of John Scarborough and Eunice Hiatt, , Charity was the three times great-aunt of Jay Hannah, was born in Salem, IA on Mar 12, 1859 and married Marion McVey (with whom she had 7 children: Essie, Estle, Guy John, Melvin Marion, Grover C, Forest L and Eunice Electa) in Henry Co., Iowa, USA on Sep 24, 1879.

She died on Sep 17, 1947 in St. Francis Hosp., Burlington, IA.
