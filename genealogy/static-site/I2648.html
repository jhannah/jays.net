Mary Hiatt (née Erwin) 1781 - 1859

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The child of Samuel Erwin, Mary was the 4 times great-grandmother of Jay Hannah, was born on Apr 9, 1781 and married Isom Hiatt (with whom she had 10 children: Jesse, William, Samuel, Charity, David Isom, Ruth L, Elizabeth Betsy, Eunice, Anna and William Lindsey) in Guilford Co. , NC on Oct 17, 1799.

She died on Feb 11, 1859 in IA.
