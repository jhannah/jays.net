Alexander McDowell 1735 - 1817

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

Alexander was the 6 times great-grandfather of Jay Hannah, was born in ?Ulster, Ireland/Virginia? in 1735 and married Jane (with whom he had 4 children: Jane, Alexander, Mary and Martha) around 1772.

He died on Feb 20, 1817 in Simpsonville, Shelby Co., Kentucky.

Children

  1. Martha was born c. 1782

Notes

WILL: Abstract by Colket. Beloved wife Darcus to live in the mansionhouse (if) she cannot have enjoyment there, there must be built a homefor her comfortable accommodations. She to have an equal part in thestock to be divided between herself and her three daughters. Householdfurniture to be distributed in same manner except bedding of whichCatherine is to have no share, and furnishing our Son John with onecommendable bed with furniture. Land to be equally divided between ourtwo sones James and John, the latter to have the manor house; alsoFarming equipment equally divided. Debt from Scott if collected to beequally divided among legatees above mentioned, James being paid forlabor on house. My son Alexander, and daughters Mary Hankins, JaneWasson and Martha Ragsdale, Ten shillings each. Bland W. Ballard andAlexander Wasson, Executors. 18 Mar 1815 Alexander McDowel. AcknowledgedIsaac Collet, Walter White, Jas Logan, proved March court 1817.

Alexander, born abt. 1735, died 20 Feb. 1817 Shelby Co, Ky, buried in theMcDowell Cemetery near Simpsonville, Shelby Co, Ky. (I believe he was inJefferson Co 7/17/1789 tax, and in Shelby Co by 8/7/1800). His will waswritten 18 March 1815 and probated March 1817 (Will Book 3, page 542Shelby Co). His wife Darcas, born abt 1745, died 7 May 1833 and is buriedin the McDowell Cemetery also. Their nine children were:

1. Jane, born abt 1773 Va, married 14 oct 1797 Shelby Co, Ky, AlexanderWaison/Warson

2. Mary, married 17 Dec 1798 Shelby Co, Ky, Clairborn Hawkins

3. Alexander JR, married 11 May 1804 Shelby Co, Ky, Crotia Millis

4. Martha, married 11 Nov 1805 Shelby Co, Ky, Richard Ragsdale

5. James (may be the James who married 26 June 1823 Shelby Co, MaryBailee)

6. Catherine

7. Elizabeth

8. Dorcas

9. John, born abt 1788, died 26 Aug 1872, buried in the McDowellCemetery, married Martha ______, born 26 Feb 1796, died 1 Aug 1851.

Alexander, his wife Dorcas, son John, and daughter-in-law Martha are theonly four McDowells buried in the cemetery.

This is all the information I have on this family. I hope it helps. Ifyou ever find a connect with Alexander and our Charles, I wouldappreciate hearing from you.
