Elizabeth Stark (née Schlief) 1867 - 1937

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The 7th of 10 children of Samuel Schlief and Katherine Hoffman, Elizabeth was the great-great-grandmother of Jay Hannah, was born in Franklin, Wisconsin on Apr 18, 1867 and married Hiram Stark (with whom she had 8 children: Girtha Cleo (Girthie), Mary Elva (Elvie), Grace, Hiram Sylvester (Vester), Henry Harley, Baby, Nellie and Loren Felix) in Fairfield, Iowa on Jan 1, 1884, which is also where she died on Jan 1, 19371.
