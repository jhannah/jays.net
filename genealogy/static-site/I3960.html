Oliver Woolman 1846 - 1926

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The younger of 2 children of Abraham Woolman and Malinda BogueOliver was the third cousin three-times-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in Salem, Henry Co., IA on Oct 10, 1846 and married Rebecca Mills (with whom he had 10 children: Mary Lena, Myrtle B, Marietta, Oscello, Otto, Grace, Maggie, Fred, Jessie and Winnie Marie) in Henry Co., IA on Nov 8, 1869.

He died on Mar 17, 1926 in Gebo, Hot Springs Co. WY and was buried in Riverside Cem., Thermopolis, Hot Springs Co., WY after Mar 17, 1926.
