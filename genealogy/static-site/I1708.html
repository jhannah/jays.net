Gulielma Bond (née Bunker) 1848 - 1913

The 7th of 9 children of Robert Bunker and Mira Dillingham, Gulielma was the three times great-aunt of Jay Hannah, was born in Henry Co., IA on May 8, 1848 and married William Bond (with whom she had 1 child, Gilbert) in Henry Co. on Jun 18, 1889.

She died on Sep 15, 1913 in Jackson Twp., Henry Co., IA and was buried in Salem South Cemetery after Sep 15, 1913.
