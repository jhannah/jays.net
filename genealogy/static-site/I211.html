(John) Charles Grimes 1761 - 1830

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The son of Jacob Grimes (Kreim) and Christina Cordula(john) charles was the 6 times great-grandfather of Jay Hannah, was born in York or Adams Co., Pennsylvania on Jan 24, 1761 and married Barbara Frank (with whom he had 10 children: George, Susannah, Jacob, Sarah, Christina, Elizabeth, Margaretta, Phillip, Rebecca and Andrew (Andreas)) around 1786.

He died on Feb 2, 1830 in Rowan Co., North Carolina1.
