George Allen Scarborough 1857 - 1943

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The son of John Scarborough and Eunice Hiatt, George was the three times great-uncle of Jay Hannah, was born in Henry Co., IA on Aug 19, 1857 and was married 3 times - to Eunice Pickett (on Oct 19, 1891 in Henry Co) Rosa Denney (on Aug 18, 1898 in Henry Co., Iowa, USA) Emma McFarland (in Jun 1919). He had 4 children with Eunice Pickett: Bessie, Ina, Ida and Bertha.

He died on Mar 3, 1943 and was buried in Salem South Cemetery after Mar 3, 1943.
