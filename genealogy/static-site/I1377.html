Mary Margaret Short (née Byers) 1839 - 1921

The daughter of John Byers and Mary Daily, Mary was the three times great-aunt of Jay Hannah, was born in Huntington, IN on Nov 17, 1839 and married Thomas Short (with whom she had 2 children: Ella Frances and John W) in Mt. Pleasant, Iowa, USA on Apr 28, 1864, which is also where she died on Jan 18, 1921.
