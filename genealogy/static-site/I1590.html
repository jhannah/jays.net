Mt. Pleasant News, Feb. 15, 1943

Marion Harshbarger

Taken By Death

Salem - Marion Harshbarger, 80, passed away at his home Sunday eveningabout 5:30. The body was brought to the Stevens Funeral Home in Salem.

Funeral services will be held Tuesday afternoon at 2 o'clock form theSalem Methodist church with the Rev. B. G. Hankins officiating. Burialwill be in the new addition to the East cemetery.

Mr. Harshbarger is survived by his wife, Mrs. Etta Harshbarger, and twodaughters, Mrs. Ray Trueblood near Salem, and Miss Viola Harshbarger, whoteaches in the schools at Wyman.
