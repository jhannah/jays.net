Mattie Parsons (née Bunker) 1880 - 1959

The daughter of Jonathan Bunker and Mary Seaton, Mattie was the first cousin three-times-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in Jackson Twp., Henry Co., IA on Oct 3, 1880 and married George Parsons (with whom she had 5 children: Ada May, Charles Alvin, Effie Maria, Ruth Amy and Georgia Marian) in Kildare, OK on Feb 24, 1901.

She died on May 18, 1959 in Arkansas City, Cowley Co., Kansas.
