Lydia Hoggatt (née Hiatt) 1747 - 1790

The child of George Hiatt and Martha Wakefield, Lydia was the six times great-aunt of Jay Hannah, was born in Frederick Co., VA in 1747. She was married twice - to Uriah Hunt (on Oct 18, 1775 in New Garden MM, Guilford Co., NC) Samuel Hoggatt (on May 5, 1784 in New Garden MM, following the death of Uriah on Aug 1, 1781).

She died on Apr 16, 1790 in Guilford Co. , NC.
