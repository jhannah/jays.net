Hiram Leonard Byers 1874 - 1956

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The 3rd of 12 children of Perry Byers and Ruth Scarborough, Hiram was the great-great-uncle of Jay Hannah, was born in Henry Co., IA on Sep 7, 1874 and married Gertrude Beery (with whom he had 7 children: Perry J, Ruth Amelia, Ada Blonde, Mabel Genevieve, Walter Leonard, Francis Leona and Martha Elizabeth) on Dec 22, 1897.

He died on May 3, 1956 in Henry Co., Iowa, USA.
