Sarah Pickard (née Hannah) 1864 - 1958

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The 5th of 9 children of John Hannah and Catherine Seton, Sarah was the three times great-aunt of Jay Hannah, was born in Henry Co., Jackson Twp on Nov 4, 1864 and married George Pickard (with whom she had 5 children: Catherine, Frank Leslie, James Owen, Harlan Evan Dewey and Jennie Hannah Myrtle) in Henry Co., Iowa, USA on Nov 26, 1885.

She died in Henry Co. on May 13, 1958.
