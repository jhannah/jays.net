Bertha Lee McDowell (née Hannah) 1894 - 1990

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The 2nd of 6 children of John Hannah and Viola Courter, Bertha was the first cousin three-times-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in Henry Co., 2 mi. S. of Winfield on Apr 2, 1894 and married Thomas 'Harry' Henry Harrison McDowell (with whom she had 5 children: Ruby Leah, Irene, Harry Rex, Robert Lee and Lois Belle) in Morning Sun, IA @@ Hannah home on Apr 23, 1916.

She died on Nov 3, 1990 in Moravia, Calif. bur. E. Cmty., Salem, IA and was buried in Salem East Cemetery after Nov 3, 1990.

I taught country school . Was a housewife after my husbands death. Thenworked as an aide in Memorial Hospital in Mt. Pleasant, Ia for sevenyears. I retired and moved to Monrovia , Calif.
